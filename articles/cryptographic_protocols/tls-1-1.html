TLSv1.1 is a revision to the TLSv1. and was released in April of 2006. Just like previous revisions to TLS, this revision aims at improving the security of the protocol by improving the encryption and hashing standards. One of the major change was to place in corrections/fixes to prevent a certain type of attack called Cipher block chaining (CBC) attacks.
